How it implements: one token set, five hues, each a triad — accent (the left bar), fill (the hue at low opacity over the surface, which mutes it), and title / body (saturated + dimmed). Both themes carry the same role names with different values, so a card just names its hue and the active theme paints it. Hue lives in the day's .yawp.json sidecar. Violet is reserved as the identity / current accent.
